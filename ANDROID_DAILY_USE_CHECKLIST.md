# ANDROID DAILY USE CHECKLIST

## Install and launch
- Confirm the app installs on the target phone.
- Launch from a cold start.
- Background the app and reopen it.
- Confirm no startup crash or blank screen appears.

## Onboarding and navigation
- Complete or skip onboarding.
- Use back navigation across deep screens and modals.
- Confirm dialogs and sheets dismiss naturally.

## Tasks and goals
- Add, edit, archive, restore, and delete a task.
- Open task detail and add a note and a resource.
- Add a goal and open goal detail.
- Confirm notes/resources remain usable with the keyboard open.

## Timetable and scheduling
- Add at least one timetable slot.
- Generate the next 7 days of schedule.
- Confirm planned sessions render correctly on Today.
- Confirm no obvious overflow appears on compact widths.

## Focus session
- Start a focus session from Today.
- Pause, resume, complete, and cancel.
- Confirm returning to the app preserves sensible state.

## Quick capture and search
- Open Quick Capture and type with the keyboard visible.
- Open Capture Inbox and edit one item.
- Open Global Search and confirm results are scrollable and tappable.
- Open the Command Palette entry point available on mobile and confirm it fits on screen.

## Review and analytics
- Open Weekly Review and verify cards/sections are readable on phone width.
- Open Analytics and confirm the header actions and charts are usable without clipping.

## Backup and export
- Create a JSON backup.
- Cancel one save action intentionally and confirm the app stays stable.
- Export an `.ics` calendar file.
- Confirm success/failure messages are readable and not raw exceptions.

## Notifications
- Open notification settings.
- Trigger the notification permission flow.
- Deny permission once and confirm the app still works.
- If permission is granted, send a test notification and confirm delivery.

## Final sanity
- Reopen Today, Tasks, Timetable, Goals, Settings, Backup, and Sync.
- Confirm no screen crashes after the full phone pass.
